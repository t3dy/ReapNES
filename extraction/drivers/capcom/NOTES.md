# Capcom Sound Driver — Research Notes

## Status: Not Started

## Overview

Capcom's NES sound driver evolved across the Mega Man series and was reused in several other titles. Notable for complex envelope systems and multi-speed tick support.

## Known Characteristics (from community research)

- Multi-speed ticks: engine supports variable speed settings within a song.
- Complex volume envelopes with sustain and release phases.
- Pitch envelopes used for vibrato and slide effects.
- Subroutine-style song data: patterns can call sub-patterns.
- DPCM usage in later titles (Mega Man 3+).

## Target Games for Investigation

1. Mega Man 2 (1988) — iconic, well-studied soundtrack
2. DuckTales (1989)
3. Mega Man 3 (1990) — introduces DPCM
